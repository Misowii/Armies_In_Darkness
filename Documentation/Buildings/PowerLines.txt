Power lines transfer power from power plants to buildings that require power(Barracks,lights,...)
	-Lazers connect power lines to the surounding buildings.
		-Lazers can be blocked by enemy units.
		-Deal damage to enemy units that block lazers based on how long they have been blocking.
		-Allied units do not block lazers. Power supply transfers through the units life support.
	-Produce a small amount of light around construction.
	***Can charge power storage trucks
